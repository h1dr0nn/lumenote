# Lumenote

**Clear notes. Calm thinking.**

Lumenote is a fast, local-first markdown note-taking application designed for focus and clarity. Built with a philosophy that prioritizes your content, it offers a distraction-free environment for writers, thinkers, and developers.

## Features

### Markdown First

Your notes are stored as pure Markdown files. No proprietary formats, no lock-in. What you write is exactly what you get, ensuring your data remains yours forever.

### Local & Private

Lumenote runs locally on your machine. Your data is stored in a high-performance SQLite database on your device, ensuring privacy and speed without relying on cloud services.

### Calm Interface

Designed to be "Calm" and "Writer-first". The UI stays out of your way, providing a clean, polished, and polished experience that helps you maintain flow.

### Seamless Sync (Self-Hosted)

Need your notes everywhere? Lumenote supports a robust self-hosted sync server. Keep your devices in sync with complete control over your data infrastructure.

### Performance

Built with **Tauri** and **Rust**, Lumenote is incredibly lightweight and fast. It launches instantly and handles large notebooks with ease.
